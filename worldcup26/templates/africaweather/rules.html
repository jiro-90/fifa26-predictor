Rules

Africaweather pool scoring

Group top two

Pick the top two finishers in every group, in order.

Exact position: 3 points.

Off by one position: 1 point.

Anything else: 0 points.

Every group pick in the room locks at the single deadline chosen by the room creator.

Final top three

Pick the final 1st, 2nd, and 3rd placed teams.

Exact position: 4 points.

Off by one position: 3 points.

Off by two positions: 1 point.

Anything else: 0 points.

The top-three pick uses the same room deadline as the group picks.

Team format

Each team has its own team name, password, and member list with real names and gamer names.

Use the same team password when returning to the room later.

Visibility

Other teams' predictions stay hidden until the room deadline has passed.

Once that single deadline passes, everyone can compare predictions and points in the room.
